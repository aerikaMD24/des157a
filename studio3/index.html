Game On

Rules

There are two players: Big Cheese and Lil Tooth. The player whose turn it is will shake the tree for apples. The number of apples will be recorded in the apple baskets. The first one to get 23 apples wins. But beware, if the tree is shaken too much, the snake might awaken and all the apples will be lost! And if the tree is shaken and there's no apples, there'll be a need to switch trees as well as player turns.

Are you ready to play?

Warning: This game has sounds. Don't play on full volume, I recommend putting it halfway at first and then adjusting accordingly
