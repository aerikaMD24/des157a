User Test Results

Significant Observations

  1. Need for clearer conext of Project Narrative: While users enjoyed their tasks, they did not understand the purpose or significance behind them
  2. Need for feedback when user completes a minigame: User wanted a 'well donee' or 'congrats' whn they accomplished the task of clearing the red circles
  3. Need for ability to escape certain 'views' in minigames: Users ended up stuck in some areeas of the website due to a lack of back button or escape

Update Plan

I plan to take all these feedbacks into account when coding my game. I first plan to full realize the concept and narrative of the project so that I can better deliver that conteent to my users and help them understand who they are in the story. I then plan to code in places of feedback for when the users carry ou the activites so users can feel ensured their doing things right. Lastly, I plan on making sure the website is sufficiently navigationable so that users to not get stck or lost when on my site.
